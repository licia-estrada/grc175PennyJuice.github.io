OUR STORY

Penny Juice of America was founded by our family in 2001. Today, we have over forty years of experience in the beverage industry, and we use that expertise to create the best experience possible for our customers and their needs. We know firsthand how important it is to have a reliable product you can trust for the kids in your care.

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to provide children with healthy beverages every day. We’ve worked with experts to make sure our 100% fruit juice concentrates contain all the vitamins and nutrients growing children need. We know how stressful it is to run childcare centers, so we’ve also made our products cost-effective and as easy as possible to use and store.
